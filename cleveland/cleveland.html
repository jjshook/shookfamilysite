The Cleveland Family

Louise Gussie "Gussie" (Patin) Cleveland was my great aunt. Hence, the Patin family, and, thus, the Cleveland family are related to (my) Shook family via marriage. Both Gussie and her husband, Captain Elmer P. Cleveland, are buried with other Patin family members (i.e., her father, mother, brother, and others) in the All Saints Mausoleum, Greenlawn Cemetery, Port Arthur, Jefferson County, Texas.
