Obiturary

MRS. LELLIA PATIN

Mrs. Lellia Patin, 88, of 3039 13th St., died at her residence following an eight-month illness.

A native of Lafayette, La., she had lived in Port Arthur since 1915. Mrs. Patin was a member of St. James Catholic Church.

Funeral services are set for 10 a.m. Monday in St. James. Msgr. Martin N. Enderle will officiate. Burial will be in All Saints Mausoleum in Greenlawn Memorial Park beside her husband, Sidney Patin, who died here in 1927.

A rosary is set for 7 p.m. Sunday in the Grammier-Oberle 39th Street Chapel.

Survivors include one son, Chester Patin; four daughters, Mrs. Irene McFadden, Mrs. Gussie Cleveland, Mrs. Eula Hickey and Mrs. Wilda Blanchard, all of Port Arthur; nine grandchildren; and 22 great-grandchildren.

Serving as pallbearers will be A. A. Hebert, Rauoul Begnaud, Phil Cleveland, Gene Hickey, James Burton and Jerry DeLaune. Honorary pallbearers will be Harris Hickey, Louis Palumbo Sr., Dr. Jan J. Shook Sr. and Mark Majors III.

Grammier-Oberle Funeral Home, 4841 39th St., 962-4408.
