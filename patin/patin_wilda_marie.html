Obituary

Wilda Blanchard

Wilda Blanchard, 73, of Port Arthur died Saturday at Oak Grove Nursing Home after an illness.

She was native of Lafayette, La., and was a longtime Port Arthur resident. She was a member of St. Peter's Catholic Church.

Graveside services will be at 10 a.m. Monday at Greenlawn Memorial Park. Arrangements are under the direction of Levinston Funeral Home in Groves.

Survivors include two daughters. Barbara Scaff of Port Arthur and Susan Schmidtke of Bridge City: two sisters, Irene McFaddin of Port Arthur and Eula Hickey of Beaumont: four grandchildren: and two great-grandchildren.
