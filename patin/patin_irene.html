Marriage Notice

Miss Irene Patin weds Ted Burton The Port Arthur News, April 9, 1933

Miss Irene Patin of Port Arthur and Ted Burton of Winchester, Texas were married in Lake Charles, La., Thursday. Mr. and Mrs. C. J. Patin of this city and Mrs. M. Bertrand of New Orleans, La., were the only attending.

Mr. and Mrs. Burton will make their home in Winchester, where Mr. Burton is engaged in business. Mrs. Burton is the daughter of Mrs. Lellian Patin of 1260 Ninth Street and was reared and educated here.

Thursday night upon their return to Port Arthur, Mr. and Mrs. Burton were honored with a wedding dinner at the home of Mrs. E. P. Cleveland on 11th Street. Guests were Maxie Patin, Mesdames M. Bertrand of New Orleans, La., and Lillian Patin; and Messrs. and Mesdames C.J. Patin, S. Casper, Harry Hickey, and Donald Blanchard.
