Obituary

Chester "Pat" James Patin, 77, of Port Neches, died Sunday at Mid-Jefferson County Hospital.

He was a retired supervisor for Texaco and a native of Lafayette, La. He had lived in Port Arthur since 1915.

Services are set for 2 p.m. Tuesday at the Grammier-Oberle Funeral Home Chappel with burial in Greenlawn Memorial Park. A rosary is scheduled to be recited at 7 p.m. Monday at the funeral home chapel.

He is survived by his wife, Helen Patin of Port Neches; three daughters, Judith Palumbo of Nederland, Gayle [sic] Shook and Sylvia Majors, both of Port Neches; three sisters, Irene McFaddin of Port Arthur, Eula Hickey of Beaumont and Wilda Blanchard of Port Arthur.
