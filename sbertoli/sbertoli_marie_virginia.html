Obiturary

March 31, 2007

Marie V. Corsiglia, nee Sbertoli, beloved wife of the late John R.; loving mother of Robert J. (Emilie), Richard J. (Barbara) and John J. (Nancy) Corsiglia; cherished grandmother of Nancy (Mark) Tomassoni, Tom (Candy) Corsiglia, Karen (Jan) Shook, Beth (Doug) Gill, Diane (Scott) Miller, Sue (Bob) Furlen, Kristen (Shane) Otis, Erika (Ihor) Andrushko and Kristin Nyberg; 15 great-grandchildren; and many loving nieces, nephews and friends. Visitation 3 to 9 p.m. on Monday at Grove Memorial Chapel, 1199 Arlington Heights Road, Elk Grove Village. Funeral Mass 10 a.m. Tuesday at Queen of the Rosary Church. Entombment Queen of Heaven Cemetery. 847-640-0566

A funeral service for Marie V. Corsiglia (nee Sbertoli), 95, took place on Tuesday, Apr. 3, at Queen of the Rosary Church. Mrs. Corsiglia died on Thursday, Mar. 29, at Manor Care Nursing Center in Elk Grove. She was a loving homemaker, and member of the Alter & Rosary Society of St. Angela Church and The Daughters of Isabella. Mrs. Corsiglia was survived by her children Robert J. (Emilie), Richard J. (Barbara) and John J. (Nancy), grandchildren, 15 great grandchildren, as well as many nieces and nephews and friends. She was preceded in death by her husband John R. and siblings John, Joseph and Edward (Edna) Sbertoli.
