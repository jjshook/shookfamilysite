The McDaniel Family

Clarkey Lucy (McDaniel) Oliver Pate (the matriarch of the Pate family) is buried in the Pate Cemetery (Pate-Sanders Cemetery). The headstone is a double marker around the middle of the cemetery. It is in rather poor condition and easy to miss (or ignore). Quite ironic, since nearly everyone buried in the Pate Cemetery is a descendent of Clarkey.

As shown in the pedigree table below, Clarkey was my great-great grandmother.
