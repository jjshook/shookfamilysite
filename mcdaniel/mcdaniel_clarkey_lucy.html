History

The 1860 Federal Census for Jackson County, Florida records Clarkey (age 18) living at her mother's house with her first husband William Oliver (age 21) and their first child Jennie (9 months). Clarkey's mother, Mary McDaniel (age 40) and William Oliver were both listed as farmers. Clarkey's siblings were recorded as George Samuel (age 16), Barbara A. (age 14), James E. (age 13), Mary A. (Polly) (age 11), Sampson (age 6), and Nancy J. (age 3).
