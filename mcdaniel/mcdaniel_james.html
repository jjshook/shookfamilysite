History

"Comments: The lineage from Daniel down to Lumpkin is taken from the tombstone of James A. McDaniel, b. Nov 12, 1924, still living, in the Dykes Cemetery, Sneads, Jackson Co., FL. The tombstone reads Jim, the son of Oscar, son of Robert, son of Lumpkin, son of Risdon, son of John M., son of James, son of Daniel, b. 1690 d. 1734. No more info on tombstone."
