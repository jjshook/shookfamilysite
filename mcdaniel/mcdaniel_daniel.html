McDaniel, Daniel

Surname McDaniel
Given Name Daniel
Sex Male
Born ~1690 in Glengarry, Scotland
Died Between 16-APR-1733 and 02-MAY-1734 in Bertie Precinct, North Carolina
Emigration 1716 to Powhatan, Virginia (on the ship "Scipo")

Marriage

1712 in Chowan County, North Carolina to Sarah BROWNE; born: About 1692 in Isle of Wight, Virginia

Children

  • Nicholas MCDANIELL; born: 1714 in Chowan County, North Carolina
  • James MCDANIELL (~1717 - bef. 29-NOV-1759 )
  • Archibald MCDANIEL; born: 1719 in Chowan, North Carolina (spouse: Joan UNKNOWN)
  • Daniel MCDANIEL, Jr.; born: About 1720 in Bertie County, North Carolina
  • Alexander MCDANIELL; born: About 1721 in Bertie County, North Carolina
  • Mary MCDANIELL; born: About 1723 in Bertie County, North Carolina
  • Ann MCDANIELL; born: About 1725 in Bertie County, North Carolina
  • Sarah MCDANIELL; born: About 1727 in in Bertie County, North Carolina; died: JUL-1797 (spouse: John OLIVER)

History

"Comments: The lineage from Daniel down to Lumpkin is taken from the tombstone of James A. McDaniel, b. Nov 12, 1924, still living, in the Dykes Cemetery, Sneads, Jackson Co., FL. The tombstone reads Jim, the son of Oscar, son of Robert, son of Lumpkin, son of Risdon, son of John M., son of James, son of Daniel, b. 1690 d. 1734. No more info on tombstone."
