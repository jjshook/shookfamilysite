Notes

This genealogy is a bit speculative, as there are few supporting records. The 1900 U.S. Census in San Jacinto County, Texas records Lloyd Sanders at 15 with a birth date of MAR-1885. Some family tree work done on Ancestry.com for Nora Lee Sanders lead s to the headstone, which has the birth date of Lloyd as 22-MAY-1885. Note May rather than March, but the birth month / year is often wrong (or cryptic) on the census. So the connection is plausible but not certain. Information about the children is also reasonable but speculative as well.
