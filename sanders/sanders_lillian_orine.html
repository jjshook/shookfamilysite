Sanders, Lillian Orine

Surname Sanders
Given Name Lillian Orine
Sex Female
Born 25-APR-1929 in Montgomery County, Texas
Died ~1930 - ~1931 in Montgomery County, Texas
Father Rufus Fred SANDERS (08-OCT-1899 - 31-MAR-1984)
Mother Christine E. WOOD (01-SEP-1904 - 06-DEC-1981)

Story

Rufus Fred Sanders was born October 18, 1899, and died March 31, 1984. Rufus and Mrs. Sanders had one child, who died as an infant.

Notes

I have not been able to find a death certificate, burial, or headstone for Lillian. She is in the 1930 but not the 1940 census (confirming her death as a child). While Lillian's father, Rufus, is buried in the Pate Cemetery, Lillian does not appear to be buried there (unless in an unmarked grave). Christine is buried with her first husband in the Oklahoma Cemetery (not far from the Pate Cemetery) as Christine E. Sanders Cross.
