The Oliver Family

Earl Oliver, Henry Washington Oliver, Mose Washington Oliver, and Nellie Diane Oliver are all buried in the Pate Cemetery (Pate-Sanders Cemetery).

Mose and Jennie Oliver were the first two children of Clarkey Lucy (McDaniel) Oliver Pate (the matriarch of the Pate Family). All of the Olivers buried in the Pate Cemetery are descendents of Mose. I have not been able to find much on Jennie.
