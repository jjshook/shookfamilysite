Obituary (Mary Enola)

Miss Mary Enola Hines, aged 21 years, died Thursday evening at 7:15 o'clock at the home of her grandmother, Mrs. M. E. Norris, 913 South Tenth. Death resulted from pneumonia and complications following influenza.

Miss Hines had been raised in Waco and was well known here. She graduated from Waco high school with the class of 1917.

She is survived, besides by her grandmother, by two sisters, Miss Katie Hines and Mrs. R. L. Stewart of Waco.

Funeral services will be held at the residence Friday afternoon at 4 o'clock with Rev. Layton Maddox of the Tabernacle Baptist church officiating, and interment will be at Oakwood.

-- Waco News Tribune, 27-FEB-1920 (findagrave.com)
