Notes

Pruda Marie Scott was born on May 17, 1939 in Lexington, Lee County, Texas and died on September 16, 1956 at the age of 17 years from an automobile accident. Her death certificate and headstone records her surname as Cross (and married). She is buried at the Pate Cemetery in Montgomery County, Texas near hear mother Fannie Lee (Sanders) Scott Sansom.
