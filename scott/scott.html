The Scott Family

Pruda Marie (Scott) Cross and Wesley Willard Scott were the children of Fannie Lee (Sanders) Willard Sansom Campbell. Pruda, Wesley, and Fannie Lee are all buried in the Pate Cemetery (Pate-Sanders Cemetery) along with Fannie's second husband Oliver Calvin Sansom, Sr..

Ella Marie (Scott) Pate was the wife of Andrew Lidden Pate (i). Ella and Andrew are both buried in the Pate Cemetery (Pate-Sanders Cemetery).

At this time, I am assuming that Pruda Marie (Scott) Cross and Wesley Willard Scott are one Scott family and Ella Marie (Scott) Pate is another Scott family, but they could be the same Scott family. Regardless, Ella is related to the Pate family, Pruda and Wesley are related to the Sanders family, and they are all associated with Pate-Sanders.
