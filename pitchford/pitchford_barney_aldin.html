Obituary (Dorothy)

Dorothy Cornelia Roberts Pitchford, age 78 of Waller, Texas passed away on July 19, 2010.

She was preceded in death by her husband Barney Pitchford.

She is survived by her son Coy Pitchford and wife Joan; daughters, Linda Craven and husband Tom, Barbara Schaefer, Lenora Wooley and husband Darrell; sister, Irma Moehle and husband James; grandchildren, Georgia Hart, Jared Pitchford, Katherine Hutson, Barbie Schaefer, Christina and Damon Wooley and others; great-grandchildren, Anne Hart, Taylor Hart, Savanna York, Cory Hutson and others.

Visitation was held on Wednesday, July 21, 2010 from 5 to 8pm at Canon Funeral Home.

Funeral Services were conducted on Thursday, July 22, 2010 at 2:30 pm at Canon Funeral Home with Pastor Steven Allison officiating.

Interment followed at Fieldstore Cemetery.

Dorothy lived to be 78 years, 11 months and 30 days old. She was just one day shy of becoming 79 years.
