Immigration

According to the 1930 U.S. Census, Joseph immigrated from Italy to the USA in 1900 (naturalized in Pennsylvania). Mary also immigrated from Italy to the USA in 1900 (naturalized in Alabama).

Obituary

Joseph Corsiglia, who operated a restaurant at Illinois and Orleans for 28 years and later at 6308 North Western Avenue, died yesterday in his home at 5338 North Western Avenue after a long illness. He was 58 years old. Mr. Corsiglia is survived by his widow, Maria; Three daughters, Mrs. Theresa Groppo, and Marie and Louise Corsiglia, and five sons John, Joseph, August, Steve and Louis.
