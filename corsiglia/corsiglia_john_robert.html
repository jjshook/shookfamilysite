Obituary

Daily Herald

Mass for John R. Corsiglia, 81, of Elk Grove Village, will be said at 10 a.m. Saturday at Queen of the Rosary Ciitholic Church, 750 Elk Grove Blvd., Elk Grove Village. Entombment will be in Queen of Heaven Mausoleum, Hillside. He died Wednesday at home. He was employed by Hyre Electric Co., Chicago, before retiring. He was a member of I.B.E.W. Local 134 and the Harmony Club of Chicago. Survivors include his wife, Marie V.; sons, Robert J., Richard J. and John J.; brothers, Joseph and Lewis, seven grandchildren; and two greatgrandchildren. Visitation will be 3 to 9 p.m. today at Grove Memorial Chapel, 1199 S. Arlington Heights Road, Elk Grove Village.
