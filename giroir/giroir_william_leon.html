Funeral Notice

Services for Willie Leon Giroir, 58, of 1200 Missouri, apartment 109, will be held at 2 p.m. Monday at Earthman Chapel.

Giroir, a resident of Baytown for 55 years, died Friday in a Baytown hospital. A World War II veteran, was employed at Exxon.

He is survived by one son, Duane L. Giroir of Pasadena; one stepson, William O. Bennett of Houston; one brother, Luby A. Giroir of Baytown; one step-grandson, Rhett Bennett of Houston; and several nieces and nephews.Burial will be in Garden of Gethsemani.

Arrangements are under the direction of Earthman Funeral Home.
