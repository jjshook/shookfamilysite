Immigration

According to the 1920 census, both John and Vincenza immigrated from Italy to the United States in 1889.

Obiturary

18-MAY-1919

Sbertoli - Virgina Sbertoli, age 30, beloved wife of John Sbertoli, fond mother of John Jr., Marie, Joseph, and Edward, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. John Briggi, sister of Mrs. C. Mangiastrelli. Funeral from late residence 2449 W. Superior St., Monday 9 a.m. to Holy Rosary Church, where solemn high mass will be celbrated. Autos to Mount Carmel. -- 16-MAY-1919 Chicago, Cook County, Illinois.

Notes

In John's 1918 WWI Draft Registration, he recorded his name as John Elesis Sbertoli, birth date as 06-MAR-1880, residence as 2449 Superior Street, Chicago, Illinois, born in Italy, working as a fruit buyer, and his wife is Virginia Sbertoli. In the 1910 cenus John and Vincenza can speak English. In the 1920 census, however, John and Vincenza's mother tongue is recorded as Italian.
