The Hirsch Family

Hirsch, Lloyd Pete was the son of Delta (Sanders) Hirsch who was the daughter of Lloyd Winfield Sanders. Lloyd Pete Hirsch is not buried in the Pate Cemetery (or Pate-Sanders Cemetery). He is buried in the Salem Lutheran Cemetery, Tomball, Harris Coounty, Texas.
