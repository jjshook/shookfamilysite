Notes

Albert was known as "Big Daddy" (the nickname Helen May GROGAN called her surrogate father).

Alice Aguillard Vautrain may be Albert's mother. He was living with Alice Vautrain in the 1920 census at age 43. He may have had a brother killed in WWI. See following:
