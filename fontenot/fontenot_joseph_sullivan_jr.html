Death Notice

TWO ORLEANIANS KILLED IN ACTIONAnother Louisianian, Three Mississippians Die

Joseph Sullivan (Buster) Fontenot, gunner's mate third class, United States Navy, 20-year old foster son of Mr. and Mrs. Emile Morlet, 417 South Johnson street, died July 27 at sea of acute poisoning, the navy department reported. A natvie of New Orleans, he was a graduate of Warren Eston High school and enlisted in the navy December 13, 1941.

-- The Times Picayune (no date on the newspaper clipping)

Notes

Joseph died during active duty (per military interment records).

Service ID: 644-08-55; enlisted 11-DEC-1941 in New Orleans, LA.

Listed as Gunners Mate on the U.S.S Maryland as of 31-MAR-1944, which was in port when Buster died.

Joseph was adopted.
