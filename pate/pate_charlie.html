Pate, Charlie

Surname Pate
Given Name Charlie
Sex Male
Born 20-SEP-1923 in Tomball, Montgomery County, Texas (Certificate 5479)
Died 18-JAN-1950 in Baytown, Harris County, Texas (Certificate 2467)
Buried Pate Cemetery in Montgomery County, Texas
Father Wilson PATE (01-APR-1875 - 08-APR-1933)
Mother Mary JOHNSON (29-MAY-1901 - 01-AUG-2001)

Marriage

02-SEP-1949 to Neva Cathleen WILCOX; born: 09-FEB-1933 in Neuces County, Texas

Notes

Headstone reads:

Charlie PateTexasCPL 656 Field ARTY BNWorld War II9/20/1923 - 1/18/1950

Charlie's death certificate records that he was hit by a passing automobile on road. He died of head injuries within four hours at the hospital. Also note that Charlie's father Wilson provided the information for the death certificate and that Charlie is recorded as "Charley Pate Jr." Subseqeuntly, Charlie is recorded in the Texas Death Index as Charley Pate, Jr. The headstone, however, was engraved as Charlie. The Texas Birth Index records Charlie as Charles. I have recorded the given name above as Charlie for now, which follows the name on the headstone (as I have partiicular interest in the Pate Cemetery) -- Jan J. Shook, Jr.
