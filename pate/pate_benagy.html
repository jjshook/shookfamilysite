Notes

Benagy is in Bible records as the son of Willoughby Pate.

Benagy Pate owned land near Faceville, Georgia. (Either the 1850 or 1860 census shows the correct "Ben" Pate family.) A PARKER researcher said that Benagy is in South Carolina records late in life living in the home of a sister. Daughter, Miranda, was with him.

Benagy Pate was living near Bainbridge, Georgia on the 1870 Census.

1880 Marlboro Co, SC census -- ED 104/17, Adamsville.
