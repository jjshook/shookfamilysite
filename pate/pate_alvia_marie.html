Obiturary (Daughter)

Darla Jeane Rudel, age 71, passed away peacefully into the arms of her loving Savior Jesus Christ on June 10, 2013 with family and friends by her side. Darla was a native Houstonian born on July 5, 1941 to Ervin and Alvia Henrichs. She was a much loved mother, daughter, nana, sister, aunt and friend. She loved traveling and spending her most cherished years with her special granddaughter, Riley Jeane, whom she adored with all her heart. She made many lasting friendships along life's journey. She had a wonderful career and retirement with James Avery, where she developed many lasting friendships. She leaves to cherish her memories: mother, Alvia Henrichs; daughter, Shawn Rudel; son, Lance Rudel; granddaughter, Riley Rudel; brother, Deuwain Henrichs; and sister, Denise Mobley. She is and will always be the bright star in all of our hearts. Well done thou good and faithful servant. I have fought the good fight, I have finished the race, and I have kept the faith. A memorial service will be held at 1:00 pm on Friday, June 14, 2013 at The Woodlands United Methodist Church, 2200 Lake Woodlands Dr., The Woodlands, TX 77380. Those wishing to post tributes and memories to the family may do so at www.eickenhorstfuneral.com.
