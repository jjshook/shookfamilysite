Notes

When Dean Anthony Shook took notes on Tassie Pate's siblings, Ander Pate was listed as a brother. That name is probably a nickname for Andrew Lidden Pate. Moreover, an Ander is buried in the Pate Cemetery as Ander L. Pate; born: 1867; died: 1905. As a result, all references to Andrew Lidden are assumed to be Ander and vice versa

Note that the birth date of 01-NOV-1866 conflicts with the birth date on "Ander's" headstone, which is 1867.

Per Mary Ann Hormuth message board posting, Andrew resided in the Oklahoma Community of Montgomery County, Texas.

In the 1890 Montgomery County, Texas Census (compiled by Mary C. Moody), the Andrew Lidden Pate family was recorded as follows:

PATE, Andrew (1); PATE, Andrew, 12, FL. SN 82D (2); PATE, Andrew L., 33, FL, Clara, 21, TX. Ch: Wm M., 12, TX. SN 63B (3); PATE, Grover Jackson, b 9-11-1893. Fa: PATE, Andrew Liden. Mo: SMITH, Ella. Cert# OA1-75
