Obituary (Walton Leon)

Walton Leon Eichelberger, 68 of Dayton passed away on November 7, 2010 at his residence in Dayton. He was a loving father, grandfather, stepfather and special friend.

Funeral services will be held at 2:00 PM on Thursday, November 11, 2010 in the chapel of Sterling Funeral Home in Dayton. Interment will follow in Magnolia Park Cemetery in Dayton. A gathering of family and friends will be from 5:00 to 8:00 PM on Wednesday at the funeral home.

Walton was preceded in death by his parents Willis and Evelyn Eichelberger Sr.; sister, Barbara Chesney. Walton worked for many years as an Operator for Equistar. He was a member of Grace Community Baptist Church in Dayton.

Walton is survived by his son, Eugene Eichelberger and wife Lisa of Humble; daughter, Jessica Swearingen of Hardin, TX; brother, Woodrow Eichelberger; nephew, Elton Chesney and wife Joyce; stepchildren, Camie Brock and husband Dale, Jackie Harris and husband Tommy, Sam Peak and wife Phyllis; grandchildren, Joshua, Matthew and Jacob Eichelberger, Colton and Grant Swearingen; paw-paw Walt to numerous grandchildren.
