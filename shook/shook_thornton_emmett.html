Obituary (Vilma)

Vilma, 78, of Lumberton, passed away at Village Creek Rehabilitation and Nursing Home after an illness. She was born in Stanton, Texas to J.V. and Addie Beatrice Bush. Vilma retired from the city of Beaumont after twenty-five years of dedicated service. She was a member of Westgate Memorial Baptist Church and a member of the choir. Her husband of thirty-three years, Thornton Emmett Shook preceded her in death. She is survived by her daughters, Darla Morrison and husband Randy and Sharon Rienstra and her husband Richard; her son, Kyle Emmett Shook and his wife Marlot; seven grandchildren; and five great grandchildren.
