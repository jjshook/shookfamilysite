Obituary

Andy was born on December 1, 1949 and passed away on Saturday, November 1, 2014.

Andy was a resident of Olney, Texas at the time of his passing. Funeral services will be held at 4:00 p.m. Monday, November 3, 2014, in the Chapel of Lunn Funeral Home with Andy Graham, pastor of Southside Baptist Church, officiating. Interment will follow in Restland Cemetery under the direction of Lunn Funeral Home of Olney.

OLNEY — Andy Carl Shook Sr., 64, died Saturday, Nov. 1, 2014.

Services 4 p.m. Monday at Lunn Funeral Chapel. Mr. Shook was a mechanic, member of American Legion Gray Bar Post #414 and served in United States Army. Survivors include: son, Andy Jr. and wife Heather; daughters, Robin Shook, Misty Schneider, Kodye Graham and husband Matthew; brother, Woodrow; 13 grandchildren.
