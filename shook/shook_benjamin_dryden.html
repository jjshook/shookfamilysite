Death Notice

B. D. Shook's Services Set

Funeral services for B. D. Shook,76, retired farmer and former resident of the Five-in-One community, are scheduled in the Methodist Church in Iowa Park at 3:30 p.m. Wednesday and burial will be in Iowa Park Cemetery.

Mr. Shook died unexpectedly in a Wichita Falls hospital Monday. He had been in bad health for some time and was hospitalized Sunday.

Mrs. Shook, a resident of Iowa Park, farmed in the Five-in-One community for a number of years.

Mrs. Shook, the former Miss Lucy Ross, is a sister of Mrs. H. G. Price and James Ross of the Five-in-One community.

Also surviving are three daughters and two sons, Ross Shook of Denver City, Texas and W. B. Shook of Iowa Park.
