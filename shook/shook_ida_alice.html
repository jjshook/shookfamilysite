Obituary

MRS. RICHARDSON TAKEN BY DEATH

Mrs. Ida Richardson 86, died Saturday morning in Ennis Municipal Hospital after a long llness.

Mrs. Richardson, formerly Ida Alice Shook, was born in Georgia and came to Texas when a child with her parents settling in Bell County. She was married in Milam County on March 16, 1894 to D.H. Richardson. They moved to the Ennis community in 1903 and Mr. Richardson died in 1957.

Surviving her are three sons, Albert R. Richardson, Griffen Richardson, both of Ennis, Raymond Richardson, Houston; one daughter, Mrs. R. A. Garrett of Ennis; twenty-one grandchildren and a number of great grand-children.

The funeral service will be held at 10 a.m. Monday in the Keever Chapel. Burial will be in Myrtle Cemetery here.
