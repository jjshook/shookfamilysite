Birth

Jan Joseph, Shook, Sr. was born on Saturday, April 9, 1938 at 3:33 PM at Saint Joseph's Infirmary, Houston, Harris County, Texas. Dr. J.E. Clarke was the attending physician, and probably delivered Jan's older and younger brothers as well. Although Earl and Effie were thought to have lived in Port Arthur at that time, Jan's birth certificate shows the family residing in Houston, Texas.

History

Jan is called "Paw Paw Doc" by his grandchildren. This name was based on the children's (Jan, Dana, and Kristin) maternal grandfather Chester James Patin, who was called "Paw Paw". The "Doc" suffix was added in reference to grandfather as a doctor.
