Death Notice

Services are pending at Paul U. Lee Funeral Home for former Baytownian Mrs. Amanda Rebekah Eichelberger Mueck, 94, of Heritage Manor, Dayton, who died Thursday at home.

Obituary

MUECK

Services for Mrs. Amanda Rebakah Eichelberger Mueck, 94, of Heritage Manor in Dayton were held at 1:30 p.m. Saturday at Paul U. Lee Funeral Home Chapel with the Rev. Robert J. Kalb of First Methodist Church of Dayton officiating.

Mueck, a former Baytonian and a member of First Methodist Church, died at Heritage Manor at 6 p.m. Thursday. She is survived by one daughter, Mrs. W. L. Veyre of Dayton; two sons, George Walter Eichelberger of Kountze and Philip Eichelberger of Houston; one brother, A. O. Shook of Pasadena; one sister, Mrs. Ada Etie of Cloverleaf.

She is also survived by 31 grandchildren, and numerous great-grandchildren and great-great-grandchildren.

Burial was in Cedar Crest Cemetery.

Pallbearers were Herman, Walton, Francis and George Eichelberger, Lee Chesney and Johnnie Zlomke.

Arrangements were under direction of Paul U. Lee Funeral Home.
