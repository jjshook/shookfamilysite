Obituary (Sherry)

Sherry Ann Vienneau was born on December 4, 1962 in Galena Park, Texas. She went to be with the Lord on February 12, 2015.

Sherry will be remembered as a beloved mother and wife that left us way too soon and is already greatly missed. She was the mother of four beautiful children, grandmother to her precious grandson and devoted wife of 25 years to her husband, Jeff.

Sherry had a passion of giving her time as a leader, classroom mom and coach to many children that she adored and loved.

Sherry joins her two sons Alex Shook and Michael Shook with the heavenly Father. She is also preceded in death by her mother Evelyn Davis Wilkerson

She leaves to cherish her memory husband Jeff Vienneau; daughter Melissa Shook; grandson Matthew Shook; son Jeffery Vienneau; and father Orville Wilkerson.

The family will receive friends from 12:00 PM (Noon) to 2:00 PM on Monday, February 16, 2015 at Sam Houston Memorial Funeral Home in Huntsville, Texas. A service will follow the visitation at 2:00 PM on Monday, February 16, 2015 in the chapel at Sam Houston Memorial Funeral Home in Huntsville, Texas. Interment will follow the service at Brooke Cemetery in Huntsville, Texas.

In lieu of flowers please donate to, Mothers Against Drunk Drivers MADD or Wounded Warriors Project in Memory of Sherry & her boys. Memorial condolences may be made to the family at www.shmfh.com
