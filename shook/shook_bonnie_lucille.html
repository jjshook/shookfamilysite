Obituary

Bonnie L. Shook Morton, 81, of Baytown, passed away peacefully in her sleep on Tuesday, October 12, 2004.

She was a loving mother, housewife and friend, she was an active member of Central Baptist Church and loved by all her friends in her Lydia Sunday School Class.

Bonnie was preceded in death by her husband of 36 years, Joe Morton, her mother, Freddie Shook and her father, Arthur Shook.

Her surviving relatives are daughters, Jo Ann Piceynski and husband William, Janice Morton; brother, Gene and sister, Judy. The family received friends from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. on Wednesday, October 13,2004 at Earthman Funeral Home in Baytown.

Funeral Service will be at 2:00 p.m. on Thursday, October 14,2004 at Earthman Funeral Home with Reverend Eddie Norris officiating. Homegoing arrangements are under the direction of Earthman Funeral Home, 3919 Garth Road, Baytown, TX. (281) 422-8181.

-- Baytown Sun, October 14, 2004
