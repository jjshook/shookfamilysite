Shook, Leroy A. "Roy"

Surname Shook
Given Name Leroy A.
Alias Roy
Gender Male
Born ~1839 in Illinois

Family Line

Leroy A. "Roy" Shook->Unknown Shook.

My research on Roy ends here for now. All census records clearly record Roy as being born in Illinois, and he is found in Wayne County, Illinois with Mary and his first three children. Where Roy was before or after that census record is a mystery.

Given Name

There are only three references that record this individual's given name: 1) The 1880 census records his name as Roan Shook, 2) Lou (Shook) Morgan's death certificate records her father as Leroy Shook, and 3) Francis' death certificate records his father as Roy A. Shook. Two of the three names, Leroy and Roy, are similar and census records tend to be a little sloppy. So I have profiled Roy as Leroy A. "Roy" Shook.
