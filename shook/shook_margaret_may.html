Obituary

Miss Margaret Shook

Whitesboro - Funeral services for Miss Margaret May Shook, 78, retired Whitesboro school teacher, who died Monday in a McKinney Hospital, will be held at 2:30 p.m. Tuesday in the Whitesboro First Baptist Church. Burial will be in Akers Cemetery, south of Sherman.

She was born in Colin County. Miss Shook was a member of the First Baptist Church and Order of the Eastern Star.

Survivors: Two brothers, Clifford Shook and Warden Shook, and a sister, Mrs. Mary McDonald, all of Fort Worth.
