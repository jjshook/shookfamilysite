Story

Jefferson Early [Jr.] and Sinia Morris Shook and children, Mary and Early, moved to Fort Worth, Tex., after living several years in Rusk. It is recorded that he was a newsdealer in Fort Worth. The son, Early, graduated from Texas A. & M. University as an electrical engineer.
