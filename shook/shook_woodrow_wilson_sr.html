Death Notice (Woodrow)

Services for Woodrow Wilson Shook, 71, were held at the Southside Baptist church.

Mr. Shook was a veteran of the US Army Air Corps in Wold War II.

He married Eva Mae Wyatt on 25 June 1940 at Walters, Oklahoma.

Mr. Shook was a retired oil field worker and member of the Baptist church.

Survived by his wife; two sons - Woodrow W. Shook and Andy Carl Shook, both of Olney; two daughters - Frances S. Mann of Denton and Rebecca Holman of Olney; three sisters - Hester DeShane of Hobbs, NM, Sally Moore of Phoenix, AZ and Frankie Anderson of Graham; six grandchildren.

Death Notice (Eva)

OLNEY — Eva Mae Shook, 92, died Saturday, Jan. 28, in Denison, Texas.

Services will be held 10 a.m. Wednesday at the chapel of Lunn Funeral Home of Olney with Kelly Russell of First Baptist Church officiating. Burial will follow in Restland Cemetery under the direction of Lunn Funeral Home of Olney.

Mrs. Shook was a self-employed seamstress.

Survivors include two sons, Woodrow Shook, Jr. and Andy Shook, both of Olney; and numerous grandchildren, and great-grandchildren.

Visitation will be from 6 to 7 p.m. today at the funeral home.
