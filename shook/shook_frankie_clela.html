FRANKIE C. ANDERSON

Frankie Clela Anderson, 78, of Iowa Park died Friday, January 11, 1991. Services were held at 2:00 p.m. Sunday at First Baptist Church in Albany with the Rev. Lloyd Conner officiating. Burial was in Albany Cemetery.

She was born October 16, 1912 in Wise County to Andy and Mary Renfro Shook. She was a former resident of Albany.

Survivors include three grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
