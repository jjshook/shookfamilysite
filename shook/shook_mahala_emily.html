Notes

Mahala was named as a daughter (and identified as the wife of James Henry) by Jacob Shook in his will. She was bequeathed 1/5th of his estate. Mahala married James HENRY. James was born on 14 Nov 1810 in Missouri Territory. He died on 26-MAR-1895 in Arkansas. James is probably the son of the John Henry with whom Jacob and Alexander Shook came to Hempstead County, Arkansas in 1814. He was a successful farmer. The value of his real and personal property was $16,000 in 1860.
