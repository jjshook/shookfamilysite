Obituary (Doris)

Doris Marie Ubanoski, 82, of Montgomery, Texas passed away in a local hospital October 16, 2014 surrounded by her family.

Doris and her late husband, Harold, had four children and would have been married 65 years on October 1, 2014. She was a dedicated wife and mother. In her latter years, she lived with her youngest daughter, Connie, her dog, Bo, and her cat, Lacy, whom she adored.

She was preceded in death by her parents, Henry and Martha Shook, her husband, Harold Ubanoski, and her son, Gary Ubanoski. She is survived by her daughter, Dinah Reuss and husband Rick, her daughter, Debbie Bischoff and husband Mike, her daughter, Connie Ubanoski, 5 grandchildren, Ricky and wife Michelle, Randy, Brandon and wife Amanda, Ryan, and Shelby. She also had 4 great grandchildren, Connor, Hunter, Rilee, Hailee, and numerous other family members and friends.

Visitation will be held Sunday, October 19, 2014 from 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM with the rosary being at 7:30 PM at Sam Houston Funeral Home in Montgomery, Texas. Funeral services will be held Monday, October 20, 2014 at 10:30 AM at St. Mary's Catholic Church in Plantersville, Texas with Father Edward C. Kucera, Jr. officiating. Graveside services to follow at Klein Memorial Cemetery in Tomball, Texas.
