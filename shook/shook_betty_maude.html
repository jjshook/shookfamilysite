Given Name

Betty Maude Shook's first name on the death certificate is spelled as Bettie; however, her first name is spelled as Betty on her headstone (which someone had to pay for the engraving). Hence, I have recorded her name as Betty.

Notes

I have included the 1920 census record on Betty's profile, as Maud Shook is recorded. However, Betty's death certificate and headstone clear record her birth as 1924. So the Maud Shook in the 1920 census is probably not Betty Maude.
