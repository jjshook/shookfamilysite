Story

Daughter of William Lafayette Shook and Lydia Matilda Latimer, Rebecca was the youngest of 7 children. She married John Arthur Coyle Dec. 5, 1906 in Waco, Texas. She and John Arthur were the parents of 8 children, 6 who lived to adulthood.
