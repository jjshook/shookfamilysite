Notes

The birth and death dates for Walter are from his headstone (findagrave.com). Unfortunately, there is no image associated with the findagrave.com record. Moreover, there is a note that the date of death is hidden by grass with a lot of question marks around the birth and death dates. So these dates may or may not be totally correct. Assuming they are correct, it appears that Walter and his father, Hosey, died the same day. Perhaps in some tragic accident. Walter was only 5 years old when he died. Hosey was only 31.
