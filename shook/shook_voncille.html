Obituary

VONCILLE KELLOGG

Mrs. Voncille Kellogg, 85, of Waco died Friday, April 18, at her residence. She was born November 16, 1917 in Mount Calm, daughter of the late Cecil and Mary Keeton Shook. She was married to Reese Kellogg until his death in 1984. She lived many years in Houston before moving to Waco in 1981. She was also preceded in death by one sister, Novetta Farrar. Survivors include: one brother; one niece; two great-nieces; one great-nephew; two great-great-nieces; and one great-great-nephew. Graveside services were held at 3 p.m. Monday, April 21, at Rosemound Cemetery in Waco with Rev. Steve Ramsdell officiating. -- Hillboro Reporter
