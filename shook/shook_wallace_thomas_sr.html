Death Notice (Gonda)

Gonda Van Marion Shook, 99, of Beaumont died Saturday, May 31, 1997 in The Woodlands Health Care Center. A native of Rockanje, The Netherlands, she lived in the Beaumont area for 75 years. A private committal service will be followed by a memorial service at 11 a.m. Tuesday, June 3, in Wesley United Methodist Church in Beaumont under the direction of Broussard's Mortuary. Visitation will be from 6 to 8 p.m. Monday at the mortuary. Mrs. Shook is survived by: son, Dr. Wallace T. Shook of Conroe; four granddaughters, Debra Reyna and Rachel Berner of Houston, Martha Waldhofer of Willis and Lydia McMahan of Richardson and seven great-grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to Wesley United Methodist Church of Beaumont. Broussard's Mortuary, P.O. Box 5507, Beaumont, Texas 77726-5507, (409) 832-1621.

Notes

According to the 1920 census, Gonda immigrated from Holland to the United States in 1904. Immigration records show the date as 20-SEP-1910 (departing from Rotterdam and arriving at New York, New York on the Potsdam).
