Given Name

Albert is records as Albert or Albert C. Shook in every document I have found so far; However, one of Albert's descendents (Eddy Vandehey) shared his middle name as Clevert. Hence, I have recorded the given name as Albert Clevert Shook.

Death

Albert's headstone records his death as 1857. The census records are pretty consistent with his birth circa 1852 - 1853. So the headstone is almost certainly wrong. Hence, I have recorded Albert's death year as 1852.
