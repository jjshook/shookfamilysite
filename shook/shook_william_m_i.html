Death Notice

Word has been recieved in Malta of the death of William Shook at St Ansgar, Iowa. Shook lived a few miles north of Malta from 1915 to 1932 when he went to St Ansgar to make his home near his son, Elza. He was 85 years old at the time of his death. -- Phillips County News - 15-OCT-1942 (Phillips Co, MT)
