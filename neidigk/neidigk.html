The Neidigk Family

According to my research, at least 21+ burials in the Pate Cemetery are related to the Neidigk family, which is related to the Pate family via marriage. There are 13 Neidigk family (surname) members buried in the Pate Cemetery (not counting marriages and maternal descendants) as follows: Ada (Neidigk) Knight, Addie (Neidigk) Beville, Clinton Neidigk, Sr., Doris Neidigk, Emma (Neidigk) Larkin, Frederick Neidigk, Jr., Frederick Neidigk, Sr., Ida Neidigk, Jennie Neidigk, Jessie Eugenia (Neidigk) Vessels, Lester Neidigk, Nathan Roland Neidigk, and Osie (Neidigk) Carroll

There are 5+ husbands of Neidigk family members buried in the Pate Cemetery as follows: Arnett Harold Carroll, husband of Osie (Neidigk) Carroll, John Ellison Knight, husband of Ada (Neidigk) Knight, John Joseph Larkin, husband of Emma (Neidigk) Larkin, James Iris Rickett, husband of Beverely Ann Neidigk, and Clinton L. Vessels, husband of Jessie Eugenia (Neidigk) Vessels.

There are 3+ wifes of Neidigk family members buried in the Pate Cemetery as follows: Versa Louise (Vaught) Neidigk, wife of Clinton Neidigk, Sr., Eugenia "Jennie" (Pate) Neidigk, wife of Frederick Neidigk, Sr., and Flora Theopal (Brown) Neidigk, wife of Lester Neidigk.

Eugenia "Jennie" (Pate) Neidigk and Frederick "Fred" Neidigk, Sr. are the patriarch and matriarch of the Neidigk family members in the Pate Cemetery. Twelve of the Neidigk's (not included Fred and Jennie) buried in the Pate Cemetery are children of Fred and Jennie as follows: Ada (Neidigk) Knight, Addie (Neidigk) Beville, Clinton Neidigk, Sr., Doris Neidigk, Emma (Neidigk) Larkin, Frederick Neidigk, Jr., Ida Neidigk, Jennie Neidigk, Jessie Eugenia (Neidigk) Vessels, Lester Neidigk, Nathan Roland Neidigk, and Osie (Neidigk) Carroll.
