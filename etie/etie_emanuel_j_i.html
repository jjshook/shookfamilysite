Notes

Two Emanuel J. Eties lived in Southeast Texas around the same time. I have noted the two men with Roman Numerals I and II (i and ii) to track the older and younger men. The older Emanuel (i) (1872 - 1931) was born in Louisiana, whereas the younger Emanuel (ii) (~1908 - ) was born in Texas. On Ancestry.com, the Texas Death Index for the older Emanuel (i) is incorrectly attached to the younger Emanuel (ii). The Texas Death Certificate has the birth date (1872) and the death date (1931), which clearly distinquishes the two individuals.
