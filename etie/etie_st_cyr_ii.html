Notes

St. Cyr's death certificate and headstone have a birth date of 1860. The census records, however, do not match that date. It looks like St. Cyr was 3 in the 1870 U.S. Census and 12 in the 1880 U.S. Census (recorded as Cuscille). The death certificate has "DK" (don't know) recorded for the father and mother of St. Cyr. So while the death date on the death certificate can be considered correct, the birth date is suspicous (and probably incorrect). Hence, I have recorded St. Cyr's birth date as 1867.
