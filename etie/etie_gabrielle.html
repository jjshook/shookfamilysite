Obituary (Juanita)

WHITNEY - On Saturday, July 16, 1960 at 8:30 o'clock a.m., JUANITA WHITNEY, daughter of the late Gabrier Otis and Joseph Whitney, Age 77 years, A native of New Orleans for the past 69 years. Relatives and friends of the family are invited to attend the funeral on Sunday, July 17, 1960 at 3:30 o'clock p.m. from the funeral home of Jacob Schoen & Son, Inc., 327 Elysian Fields ave. (parking entrance 2223 Chartres st.). Interment at McDoughville cemetery.

Obituary (Earl)

WHITNEY - On Monday, January 24, 1966 at 4 o'clock p.m., EARL WHITNEY, husband of the late Lucy Swan; father of Alfred B. Whitney and Mrs. Lena Whitney Brupbacher; brother of Lena Whitney and the late Mrs. James McMahan, Nita and Andrew Whitney, also survived by 7 grandchildren and 10 great-grandchildren, age 77 years, a native of New Iberia, La., and a resident of Poydras, La. for the past 20 years. Relatives and friends of the family, also employees of Louisiana Department of State and County Municipal Employees AF of L-CIO Local 1712, are invited to attend the funeral. Services from the funeral home of Jacob Schoen and Son, Inc., 527 Elysian Fields Ave (parking 2223 Charles St.) on Wednesday, January 26 1966 at 11 o'clock a.m. with Requiem Mass at St. Peter and Paul Church. Interment McDoughville Cemetery. Friends may call after 7 p.m. Tuesday.
