Obituary (Margaret)

Margaret E. "Marge" Turner Etie

SAN LEON - Our beloved mother, grandmother and great grandmother Margaret E. "Marge" Turner Etie was welcomed into the gates of heaven March 6, 2004. She was born September 9, 1939, in Nashville, Tennessee.

She was proceeded in death by her parents Archie Earl and Ola Florence Wilson Turner, her sister Doris Turner Deforest, and nephew Randy Camper.

She is survived by her brothers Jerry and Bobby Turner; nephew Wally Camper; husband George Etie; daughter and son in law, Ricky and Donna Knighton; son and daughter in law Adrian and Briana Etie; son and daughter in law Sheldon and Tiffany Etie; seven grandchildren, two great grandchildren and nieces and nephews. She will be sadly missed by her best friends Georgia Jones, Keith Boyd, Louise Kettle and her life long friend Jody Taylor.

There will be a gathering of friends and family Sunday, March 28, 2004, at 3 p.m. at Hillman's Seafood for a "Celebration of Life" and to toast to her Bon Voyage.

Published March 14, 2004 [Galveston County Daily News]
