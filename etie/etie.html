The Etie Family

Etie, John Jefferson Davis Sr. married Ada Melissa (Shook) Etie.

My main interest in the Etie family relates to Ada Melissa (Shook) Etie. However, I have done some research up and around the Etie family tree. The connections to Pierre Guillame Etie are all over the place on Ancestry.com and RootsWeb.com. Almost everything is unsourced. So the entries preceding Emanuel Joachim Etie are just placeholders. Do not take anything unsourced as correct. It probably is not.
