White, William CSA

Surname White
Given Name William [D. or T.]
Sex Male
Born OCT-1830 in Lynchburg, Harris County, Texas
Died 07-OCT-1862 in Saint Louis, St. Louis County, Missouri
Buried Jefferson Barracks National Cemetery, Saint Louis, St. Louis County, Missouri
Father George Wallace WHITE (30-SEP-1800 - FEB-1850)
Mother Rebecca PHIPPS (1805 - ~1842)

Notes

Wounded at the battle of Shiloh. Captured thereafter, and died at Gratiot Street Prison, St Louis, Missouri. Buried at Jefferson Barracks National Cemetery, Saint Louis, St. Louis County, Missouri.

There are two William White records in the 1860 United States Census. One of those William White records records the birth as Missouri and the other as Texas. The 1860 census records shown below is the Texas White.
