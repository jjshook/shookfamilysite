The Carroll Family

Arnett Harold Carroll, the husband of Osie (Neidigk) Carroll, and their son Harold Loyd Carroll are all buried in the Pate Cemetery (Pate-Sanders Cemetery).

Eugene Robbins is also buried in the Pate Cemetery (Pate-Sanders Cemetery). Eugene was the first husband of Laura Lois (Carroll) Robbins Hughes.
